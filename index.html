About Me

I work on LLMs (OLMo and olmOCR) at Ai2. My days are spent implementing the latest research findings, building datasets and benchmarks, and convincing neural networks to behave. In short, I help build language models that (usually) do what they're told with minimal grumbling.

I implement latest research findings into LLMs to keep my programming muscle memory sharp.

I'm all about adventure whether it's exploring new places, experimenting in the kitchen, playing sports, writing, or getting lost in movies.

Research Interests

I strive to solve complex challenges in three crucial areas:
1. Attention Mechanisms.
2. LLM Modeling and Optimization.
3. Dataset Quality, Bias and Fact-Checking.
